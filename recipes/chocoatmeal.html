Chocolate Oatmeal Porridge

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup hot water
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup steel cut oats
  • 5 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Dissolve cocoa powder in hot water in a saucepan
  2. Add milk and bring to a boil
  3. Add salt and oats
  4. Simmer over low heat, stirring constantly, until oatmeal has thickened (about 15 mins)
  5. Remove from heat and stir in honey
